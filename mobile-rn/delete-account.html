Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account and data are deleted, you will not be able to recover any information, preferences, or account data. Please download any data you want to keep before requesting deletion.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile data
  • App Data: All data associated with react native mobile application
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics and preferences
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and session information

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)

  1. Click the email link below to open your email client
  2. Include your account email address in the message
  3. Specify what data you want deleted
  4. Send the email and wait for confirmation

Method 2: Manual Email

Send an email to privacy@mobilern.app with the following information:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Your account email address
  • Confirmation: That you understand deletion is permanent

Processing Timeline

  • Email Confirmation: You'll receive an automated confirmation within 24 hours
  • Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity
  • Deletion Process: Data will be deleted within 30 days of verification
  • Final Confirmation: You'll receive confirmation when deletion is complete

Alternative: Delete from App

You can also request account deletion directly from the Mobile RN app:

  1. Open the Mobile RN app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Look for "Delete Account" or "Account Management"
  4. Follow the in-app deletion process

Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Receive confirmation of deletion
  • Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities if needed
